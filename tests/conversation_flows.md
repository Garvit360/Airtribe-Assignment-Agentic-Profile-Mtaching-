# Conversation Flows (Manual)

## Flow 1: Initial Search
**User:** Find me candidates with React and 3+ years experience.
**Agent:** Returns top ranked list with ids, scores, and recommendations.

## Flow 2: Refinement
**User:** Actually require 5+ years and AWS.
**Agent:** Re-extracts requirements, re-ranks, and updates shortlist.

## Flow 3: Compare Top 3
**User:** Compare top 3.
**Agent:** Side-by-side comparison with recommendation order.

## Flow 4: Explain Ranking
**User:** Why did John rank higher than Jane?
**Agent:** Explains score deltas, strengths, and gaps.

## Flow 5: Interview Questions
**User:** Generate interview questions for Alice.
**Agent:** Outputs 5-7 tailored screening questions.

## Flow 6: Multi-Round Screening
**User:** Find candidates with data engineering and 4+ years experience.
**Agent:** Produces top 10, deep analysis summaries, and final hire/no-hire recommendations.
